Accommodation

If you want to stay over in the local area for the wedding, there are various accommodation options.

The most convenient accommodation is onsite at Bisham Abbey. At the time of writing, this is also the cheapest. However, there is a limited number of rooms available, so if you want to take this option, we recommend you book soon.

Bisham Abbey Rooms

Bisham Abbey National Sports Centre has around 50 newly built rooms for sports clubs. Guests can book these rooms, but we do not have exclusivity on occupancy, so they may sell out.

Cost (pr/pn): Double - £86. Single - £70. Child bed - £15. Cots £10

Location:Onsite

Note: Child beds can only put in two of the rooms. We have reserved these rooms, so let us know if you need one.

Premiere Inn, Marlow

A Premiere Inn located just the other side of Marlow Bridge as part of the George and Dragon pub.

Cost (pr/pn): At time of writing (October 17): £96 for a double room

Location:Just over a mile from Bisham Abbey

Crowne Plaza Marlow

A Crowne Plaza hotel the other side of Marlow. Has full hotel facilities

Cost (pr/pn): At time of writing (October 17): £99 for a double room

Location:About 2 miles from Bisham Abbey

Ceremony

The wedding ceremony is at 12:15 on 7th April 2018 at Bisham Church, SL7 1RW

Parking: We have arranged for use of the co-located Bisham School car park. This should provide ample parking for all guests. The school and church are clearly signposted from the main road. Once you have turned off the main road, turn immediately right through the school gates. Continue round to the front of the school for extended parking.

Reception

The reception will be following the ceremony and is at Bisham Abbey National Sport Centre.

Bisham Church and Abbey are neighbors. However as the Abbey grounds are quite large, the drive or walk along the road is around 900m. It is possible to cut across the Abbey grounds, however the grass is often wet and muddy, so not recommended; especially for high heels!

There will be a meal at around 15:30. The menu has not yet been formalised. We will email you when this is available. However, if you have any requirements (such as vegetarian food), please include this on your RSVP.

There will be light food and a ceilidh in the evening.
